Tips:

  1. To access the Options page, right-click on the DP icon in the Chrome toolbar and click "Options", or click on DP Options below.
  2. If you find these update notifications annoying, you can disable them by unticking "Show Update Popup" in the Options page.
  3. You are able to use Decreased Productivity in Incognito Mode! In a new tab, paste chrome://extensions/ in the address bar, press Enter, tick "Developer Mode", and tick "Allow in incognito" under Decreased Productivity.
  4. Want to quickly cloak a page? Press CTRL/CMD + F12 on the page (requires "Enable Key Toggling" to be enabled; enabled by default).
